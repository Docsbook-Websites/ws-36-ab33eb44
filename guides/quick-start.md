# How to Start Using Linear for Product Development

Follow these steps to set up and utilize Linear for your product development projects effectively.

## 1. Sign Up for Linear

- Visit the [Linear sign-up page](https://linear.app/signup).
- Fill in your details to create an account. You can also sign up using your existing Google account for quicker access.

## 2. Create Your Workspace

- After signing up, create a new workspace.
- Name your workspace according to your team or project.
- Invite team members by entering their email addresses. They will receive invitations to join your workspace.

## 3. Set Up Your First Project

- Navigate to the "Projects" section in the sidebar.
- Click on "New Project" and provide a name and description.
- Define the project's goals and timelines to keep your team aligned.

## 4. Add Issues to Your Project

- Go to your project and click on "Add Issue."
- Fill in the issue title, description, and assign it to a team member.
- Set the priority and due date to ensure timely completion.

## 5. Utilize Linear Agent for Automation

- Explore the Linear Agent feature to automate repetitive tasks.
- Assign issues to Linear Agent, which can help triage, plan, build, and review all in one place.
- Use the Coding Sessions feature to allow Linear Agent to write code, enhancing your development speed.

## 6. Monitor Progress with Initiatives

- Create initiatives to define high-level goals for your projects.
- Use the "Initiatives" section to track progress and ensure alignment with your company’s objectives.
- Regularly update the status of initiatives to keep your team informed.

## 7. Collaborate with Your Team

- Use the comments section on each issue to discuss tasks and provide feedback.
- Leverage the Slack integration to receive real-time updates and communicate with your team directly from your messaging platform.

## 8. Review and Adjust

- Regularly review your projects and initiatives to assess progress.
- Adjust priorities and timelines as needed based on team feedback and project developments.
- Utilize the changelog to stay updated on new features and improvements in Linear.

## 9. Explore Additional Resources

- Visit the [Linear Docs](https://linear.app/docs) for comprehensive guides and best practices.
- Join the Linear community on Slack to connect with other users and share insights.

## 10. Provide Feedback

- Share your experiences and suggestions with the Linear team to help improve the platform.
- Use the feedback option in the app or contact support for any issues you encounter.

By following these steps, you will effectively set up and use Linear to enhance your product development process, ensuring better collaboration and efficiency within your team.
