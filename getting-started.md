## What You'll Learn
In this tutorial, you will learn how to set up Linear for your product development needs, enabling you to plan, build, and ship products effectively. You will also discover how to leverage AI agents to enhance your workflow.

## Prerequisites
- A Linear account. Sign up [here](https://linear.app/signup).
- Access to Linear's latest version (ensure you are using version 1.0 or higher).
- Basic understanding of product development processes.

## Steps to Get Started with Linear

1. **Create Your Linear Account**
   - Visit the [Linear signup page](https://linear.app/signup) and fill out the registration form.
   - Verify your email address to activate your account.

2. **Set Up Your Workspace**
   - Log in to your Linear account.
   - Click on "Create Workspace" and enter a name for your team or project.
   - Customize your workspace settings according to your team’s needs.

3. **Invite Team Members**
   - Navigate to the "Team" section in your workspace.
   - Click on "Invite Members" and enter the email addresses of your team members.
   - Assign roles based on their responsibilities (e.g., Admin, Member).

4. **Familiarize Yourself with the Interface**
   - Explore the dashboard to understand the layout.
   - Check out the sidebar for navigation options like Issues, Projects, and Roadmaps.

5. **Create Your First Project**
   - Go to the "Projects" tab and click on "New Project."
   - Name your project and set a description to clarify its goals.
   - Add team members to the project for collaboration.

6. **Add Issues to Your Project**
   - Click on the "Issues" tab and select "New Issue."
   - Fill in the details, including title, description, and priority.
   - Assign the issue to a team member and set a due date.

7. **Utilize Linear for AI Agents**
   - Explore the "Linear for Agents" feature to build and deploy AI agents.
   - Use agents to automate tasks, such as triaging issues or generating reports.
   - Monitor agent performance through the dashboard.

8. **Integrate with Other Tools**
   - Go to the "Integrations" section to connect Linear with tools like GitHub, Slack, or Sentry.
   - Follow the prompts to authorize and set up integrations for seamless workflows.

9. **Start Using Coding Sessions**
   - Access the "Coding Sessions" feature to have Linear Agent assist in writing code.
   - Assign coding tasks directly to the agent for efficient development.

10. **Review and Iterate**
    - Regularly check project progress and adjust priorities as needed.
    - Use feedback from team members to refine processes and improve collaboration.
